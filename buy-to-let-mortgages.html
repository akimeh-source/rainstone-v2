BUY-TO-LET MORTGAGES

Building Your Property Portfolio

Expert Buy-to-Let Advice

If you're buying a property with the intention of renting it out, a buy-to-let mortgage is the ideal fit. Our skilled consultants possess the in-house know-how to comprehend the current challenges faced by landlords, from changing tax regulations to evolving lending criteria.

As the BTL Broker of the Year 2024, we have proven expertise in securing competitive rates for landlords and property investors. Whether you're a first-time landlord or managing a large portfolio, we provide tailored solutions that match your investment strategy.

We work with a comprehensive panel of specialist BTL lenders, including those who offer products for HMOs, multi-unit freehold blocks, limited company purchases, and portfolio landlords with 10+ properties.

HMO Mortgages

Specialist lending for HMOs

Houses in Multiple Occupation require specialist mortgage products. We work with lenders who understand HMO investments and can offer competitive rates based on room rental income rather than just the property value.

Limited Company BTL

Tax-efficient investing

Purchasing through a limited company can offer significant tax advantages for landlords. We specialise in arranging BTL mortgages for SPVs and can guide you through the benefits and considerations.

Portfolio Landlords

Managing multiple properties

Portfolio landlords with 4+ mortgaged properties face additional criteria. Our expertise ensures smooth applications and access to lenders who understand the portfolio landlord market.
